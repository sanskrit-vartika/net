About Us अस्माकं परिचयः

🪔 Our Story

Due to the lack of Sanskrit study materials in the Bengali language, we started this initiative. We regularly upload completely free UGC NET Paper-2 (Sanskrit) study materials and videos on our YouTube channel.

Our mission is simple: democratize Sanskrit education so that every student in West Bengal and all over India can access world-class preparation resources for UGC NET Sanskrit — regardless of their economic background.

संस्कृत-वर्तिका (Sanskrit-Vartika) means "the lamp of Sanskrit" — we aim to illuminate the path of Sanskrit learning for everyone.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide high-quality, free Sanskrit study materials for UGC NET preparation in the Bengali language, making Sanskrit education accessible to all regardless of socioeconomic background.

🌟 Our Vision

To become the most trusted Sanskrit education platform for Bengali-speaking students, and to keep the tradition of Sanskrit scholarship alive and vibrant in Eastern India and beyond.
