About Us

🪔 Our Story

Due to the lack of free and affordable study materials for UGC NET, we started this initiative. You will find Paper 1 & Paper 2 mock tests for UGC NET & SET exams. We regularly upload completely free UGC NET Paper-2 (Sanskrit) study materials and videos on our YouTube channel — making quality education accessible to all..

Our mission is simple: democratize education so that every student in West Bengal and all over India can access world-class preparation resources for UGC NET — regardless of their economic background.

संस्कृत-वर्तिका (Sanskrit-Vartika) means "the wick (lamp) of Sanskrit" — we aim to illuminate the path of learning for everyone.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide high-quality, free and affordable Paper 1 & Sanskrit study materials for UGC NET preparation in the Bengali language, making education accessible to all regardless of socioeconomic background.
